Short explanation

What quantum confinement means

Quantum confinement happens when a semiconductor crystal becomes so small that electrons and holes are restricted inside a nanoscale space. When the dot gets smaller, the allowed energy levels spread farther apart. That increases the band gap, so the emitted photon has higher energy and a shorter wavelength, shifting the color toward blue.

How to read it

Dot size controls display color

The circle represents the quantum dot. The horizontal bars represent simplified valence and conduction energy levels. When the dot is smaller, the separation between those bars increases. The photon arrow changes color because photon energy and wavelength are linked by E = hc/lambda.
